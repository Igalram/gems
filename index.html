Culture Gems

Art heritage collectibles marketplace

Culture Gems is a REVOLUTIONARY Blockchain-based platform that will allow art museums to offer UNIQUE, COLLECTIBLE, and TRADABLE DIGITAL tokens (Gems) of the items on their vast collection

Auctions

Gems will be offered on a continuous basis by a growing number of art museums. They will be offered both online and on premises. In singles and in closed packs. They will be the inalienable property of their collectors, with ownership secured, decentralized, immutable.
